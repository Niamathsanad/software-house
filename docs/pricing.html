Pricing FAQ

How do you estimate a fixed-price project?

We break your requirements into a Statement of Work with defined deliverables and acceptance criteria, then quote against that — so both sides know exactly what "done" means.

Can I switch models mid-project?

Yes. It's common to start a project fixed-price for the design phase, then move to time & materials once development begins and requirements get more fluid.

What's included in a retainer?

A set number of hours per month reserved for your team, covering bug fixes, small feature work, and monitoring — with a guaranteed response time.

Do you require a deposit?

Most engagements start with a percentage upfront tied to the first milestone, detailed in your SOW or MSA.
